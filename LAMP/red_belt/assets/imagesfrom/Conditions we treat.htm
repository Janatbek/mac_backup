Conditions We Treat:

  • Neck and Back pain (disc displacements, nerve impingement, radiculopathy, joint dysfunctions and degenerative joint disease, sciatica, post-surgical pain, scar tissue management)
  • Shoulder pain (i.e., impingement, rotator cuff syndrome, “frozen shoulder”, tendonitis, instability)
  • Knee pain (patello-femoral dysfunctions, DJD, ligamentous injuries, tendonitis, meniscal injuries)
  • Hip pain (bursitis, arthritis, tenosynovitis)
  • Elbow/wrist/hand pain (i.e., carpal tunnel syndrome, tennis or golfer’s elbow, neuropathy, trigger finger)
  • Ankle/foot pain (i.e., plantar fasciitis/heel spurs, Achilles tendonitis, ankle sprains, metatarsalgia, nerve entrapments)
  • Headaches
  • TMJ (temporo-mandibular joint) dysfunctions
  • Myofascial pain syndrome, soft tissue dysfunctions
  • And More ...

Our clients come to us with a wide range of conditions: some come for post-surgical rehabilitation (joint replacements, spinal surgeries, ligamentous or tendinous repair, etc.); others have been in auto or work accidents or have suffered sports injuries and need post-traumatic rehabilitation.

Some clients suffer from chronic pain, arthritic/degenerative conditions (disc, joint), Fibromyalgia, Somatic dysfunctions, or gait abnormalities.

We customize treatments for each individual.

Why Choose Us

Our Mission

At Physical Therapy Professional Center, Inc. we strive to develop individualized rehabilitation programs to treat your whole body. We provide comprehensive, quality, evidence-based treatments. You will feel at home at our beautiful facility and our smiling, happy staff will go out of its way to put you at ease. We have been taking care of the needs of the local communities for more than twelve years, and we are proud to say that word of mouth has always been our main referral source.

Holistic approach

At PTPC, Inc. we use a holistic approach to treatment utilizing combined philosophy of American and European rehabilitation. Our treatments are customized to your needs. We think of physical therapy as a team sport, and our patients immediately become a part of our team!

Our Values:

  • Care, respect and devotion to our patients
  • Focus on positive results and outcomes
  • Integrity
  • Professional perseverance

Privacy

All our hands-on treatments are conducted in the private rooms. Your gym exercises are never in a crowded environment. You will be seen by the practitioner you expect to see and the continuity of your care is guaranteed.

Great location

We are located less than a mile off of route 270, near the intersections of Clopper and Quince Orchard Roads in Gaithersburg behind the Quince Orchard Plaza Shopping Center. Parking is free, and our offices are right on the ground floor.
